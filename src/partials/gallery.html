Lion Fish

The lionfish is a venomous marine fish native to the Indo-Pacific region that has distinctive red, white, and black stripes and long, showy fins.

Whales

Whales are large aquatic mammals that live in the world's oceans and use echolocation to communicate and navigate underwater.
